# Spaghetti Carbonara

## Ingredients

- salt
- ground black pepper
- 2 potato
- 1 kg bacon strips
- 1/2 tbsp syrup
- 2 kg potato
- 4 cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 200 g flour
- 100 ml water
- 100 g cheese
- 50 g spinach
- 1 onion (peeled and finely chopped)
- 2 cloves garlic (peeled and minced)
- onion (red)

## Equipment

- pot
- potato masher
- baking sheet
- oven

## Instructions

Then add **salt** and **ground black pepper** to taste.

Poke holes in **2 potato**.

Place **1 kg bacon strips** on a baking sheet and glaze with **1/2 tbsp syrup**.

*(Don't burn the roux!)*

Mash **2 kg potato** until smooth *(alternatively, boil 'em first, then mash 'em, then stick 'em in a stew.)*

Slowly add **4 cup milk**<!-- TODO change units to litres -->, keep mixing

Place the potatoes into a *pot*.
Mash the potatoes with a *potato masher*.

Lay the potatoes on a *baking sheet* and place into the *oven*. Bake for **25 minutes**.

Boil **2 eggs** for **3 minutes**.

> Don't burn the roux!

Mash **2 kg potato** until smooth *(alternatively, boil 'em first, then mash 'em, then stick 'em in a stew.)*

### Dough

Mix **200 g flour** and **100 ml water** together until smooth.

### Filling

Combine **100 g cheese** and **50 g spinach**, then season to taste.

Mix **1 onion** *(peeled and finely chopped)* and **2 cloves garlic** *(peeled and minced)* into paste.
